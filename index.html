How It Works

UNI is the #1 app for real-life discovery, helping users uncover everything happening around them through personalized, dynamic maps powered by the world’s biggest event and location databases. Users create detailed profiles based on their favorite artists, go-to spots, college, and lifestyle preferences. Every RSVP, check-in, and interaction is encrypted and converted into real-time behavioral insights—fueling smarter recommendations for users and powering UNI’s unparalleled data solutions.
